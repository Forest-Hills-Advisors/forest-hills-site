Concept 2: The Refined Vertex

The Unified Monogram

Weaving F-H-A

By shifting the letter 'A' to form the final column on the right, we achieve a balance that feels both ancient and cutting-edge. The "Concept 2" design ensures each letter is distinct yet interwoven.

  • **Clarified Vertex:** Definitive sharp 'A' cap.
  • **Gothic Balance:** Anchored by vertical H-structure.
  • **Material Ready:** Optimized for debossing and foil.

Dual Brand Archetypes

FHA (Luxury Sales)

Persona: The Architectural Curator.

Minimal, precise, and editorial. Utilizes "Concept 2" on high-polished brass and velvet-touch materials to signal exclusivity and discretion.

FHRA (Heritage Leasing)

Persona: The Local Institution.

Rooted, historic, and detailed. Employs the fabricated station-style stencil typography to establish a "neighborhood-first" credibility.

Luxury Collateral

The business card execution utilizes a heavy, dark-green textured cardstock. The "Concept 2" monogram is debossed in gold foil, emphasizing the tactile quality of the brand.

"Design is in the detail of the forge, not just the pixels on the screen."

Heritage Signage

The Forged Esthetic

For FHRA, the "Heritage" aspect is brought to life through hammered iron and arched brackets. This environmental storytelling makes the brand feel like a permanent part of Forest Hills Gardens.

  • Material: Hammered Iron & Slate.
  • Typography: Station-style Stencil.
  • Presence: Institutional Authority.
