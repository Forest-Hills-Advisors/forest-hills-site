Brand Architecture

Realty Advisors (FHRA)

Focus: Heritage Leasing & Management

Persona: The Local Institution. Rooted, historic, and reliable. Drawing from the craftsmanship of the early 20th century to offer a "trusted neighbor" experience.

Forest Hills Advisors (FHA)

Focus: Ultra-Luxury Sales

Persona: The Modern Curator. Minimal, architectural, and precise. A refined, editorial approach for high-net-worth acquisitions.

Heritage: Realty Advisors

"A brand that feels forged, not designed."

FHRA utilizes a custom wordmark inspired by the 1906 station signage. It represents a commitment to the historical narrative of the Gardens, using wrought-iron stencils and deep forest green to establish immediate credibility.

  • LIRR-inspired Custom Typography
  • Wrought-Iron Decorative Motifs
  • Traditional Heritage Palette

Luxury: Forest Hills Advisors

For the luxury sales division, we pivot to architectural restraint. This is about precision and the luxury of negative space. The FHA mark is designed for premium applications: brass plaques, gold foil, and heavy-stock embossed paper.

"Minimalism in real estate branding is the ultimate form of confidence."

The Unified Monogram

Weaving F-H-A

The monogram serves as the connective tissue between brands. By designate a clear diagonal vertex for the 'A', we've created a mark that feels both ancient and cutting-edge.

It is balanced to work as a small-scale social icon or as a large-scale iron gateway emblem. The geometry is derived directly from the Tudor arch structures found throughout the Gardens.
