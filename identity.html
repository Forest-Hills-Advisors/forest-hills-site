Forest Hills Gardens is not generic luxury—it is an English-inspired oasis defined by Atterbury’s Tudor Revival architecture and Olmsted’s human-scaled streetscapes.

Strategic Brand Architecture

Forest Hills Advisors (FHA)

Tier: Luxury Sales & Acquisition

Positioning: Minimal, Architectural, Authoritative.

Targeting ultra-high-net-worth clients with a focus on precision and modern luxury values.

Realty Advisors (FHRA)

Tier: Heritage Leasing & Management

Positioning: Rooted, Historic, Crafted.

Establishing the firm as a "Local Institution" deeply connected to the neighborhood's story.

FHRA: Heritage & Rootedness

The FHRA brand language is "forged, not designed." It draws directly from the 1906 Forest Hills station aesthetic.

  • Typography: Custom wrought iron stencil serif.
  • Ornamentation: Tudor-inspired scrollwork and shields.
  • Vibe: Established, curated, and deeply detailed.

FHA: Luxury & Precision

The luxury sales division emphasizes restraint and architectural clarity. By stripping away ornamental competing elements, we create a sense of exclusivity.

"True luxury is found in the negative space and the quality of the materials."

The Monogram Execution

The FHA Vertex Clarity

To ensure the F-H-A letters work cohesively, we introduced a specific diagonal path to create a definitive 'A' vertex.

  • Interwoven Geometry: Distinct but unified.
  • Vertex Sharpness: Reflecting architectural precision.
  • Materiality: Designed specifically for brass casting and gold foil stamping.

The "Institution" Effect

By mimicking historic neighborhood signage, the brand bypasses "new company" skepticism. It creates an immediate perception of longevity and stability.

"The brand doesn't just represent Forest Hills Gardens; it feels like it built it."

Scalability & Digital Clarity

Digital Optimization

Heritage marks are simplified by 20% for mobile displays. We remove secondary scrollwork to ensure the stencil font remains legible at thumbnail sizes.
